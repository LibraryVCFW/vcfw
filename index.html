About Our Library 

Central Library

Seminar Library

Aside from the primary Central Library, the college possesses a seminar library with a spacious surface of 968.13 square feet in its main building. This collection is designed to cater primarily to the humanities faculty & students which offers an extensive selection of around aprx.5000 books. In addition, there are seven departmental workstations that come fully equipped with essential furnishings, as well as network computers linked to the internet and equipped with printers. Borrowing reference materials from the departmental seminar libraries is a feasible option for both educators and pupils, for a brief duration.

What is "Earn While You Learn"?

"Earn While You Learn" is a program that allows students to earn an income while acquiring valuable skills and knowledge. These opportunities often include part-time jobs, internships, freelancing, or campus employment programs. It helps students become financially independent and gain real-world experience.
