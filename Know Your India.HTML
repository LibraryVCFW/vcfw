Republic of India

Unity in Diversity

Key Facts

Area: 3,287,263 km²
Population: 1.4 billion (approx.)
Capital: New Delhi
Currency: Indian Rupee (INR)
Independence Day: 15th August 1947
Republic Day: 26th January 1950

Achievements of India

  • Chandrayaan-3: Successful lunar landing mission (2023).
  • India's Mars Orbiter Mission (Mangalyaan) - First Asian nation to reach Mars orbit (2014).
  • World's largest democracy with over 900 million voters.
  • Fastest-growing major economy in the world.
  • Home to the world’s tallest statue, the Statue of Unity (182 meters).
  • Significant contributions to the field of IT and space research.
  • Hosts the Kumbh Mela, the largest human gathering on Earth.
  • Produced global leaders in medicine, technology, and arts.
  • Ranked top in global milk production and diamond polishing.
  • Bollywood - The largest film industry in the world by ticket sales.
